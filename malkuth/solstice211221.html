Postcards from Malkuth #2

Solstice

21/12/21 Glasgow


on the darkest day of the year

a black candle burning

as our prayers rise

to the oppressive dark winter skies

bidding farewell

to that which renders the spirit blind

to time standing still in the passing of time



the phoenix dancing overhead in heavens now alight

the smoke of mugwort wormwood marigold

the gaze of the all seeing eye through the trees

my paranoid mind



I give my ashes to the earth

conjoin holly ivy pine

to make a wreath that speaks

of the grace bestowed on those who can bear

to utter goodbye

speaks

of time standing still in the passing of time
