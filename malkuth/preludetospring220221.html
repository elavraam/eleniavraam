Postcards from Malkuth #1

Prelude to Spring

22.o2.21 Glasgow

It does not make sense to me

talking about incarnations as

past or future

what was I before and what

will I come to be

for their nature is infinity

I am

already that is

the crocuses

trumpets heralding the return of Spring

and snow drops delicate and full of grace

I was tempted to say

virginal, pure

but it is all sweet contamination here on this earth

the ugly gypsy violin rising from below the hill

just how ugly, my God

its sound the very sound of a fall

self-pitying, begging

begging

I am the body of this city sprawling down below

a valley of concrete, steel and nuts and bolts

and human skulls so fraught with longing

the earth

the flesh

fire:water:air

the earth

the flesh

its symphony:its spirit

magpies blackbirds longrtailed tits

barking and baby cries

ball bouncing on concrete

the rustling of the leaves

the hiss of chaos in between

up here at the flagpole I am all

and the insistence of the Crow

my wake up call
