Art of Life Everlusting

Eleni Avraam is an artist based in Glasgow. Her work so far has treaded fantastical paths steeped in eroticism, magick and psychedelia, with a focus on desire, the occulted body and expansive consciousneas, characterised by intense colour saturation, distortion and the use of inversion and the mirror as primary tools for apocalypsis, transformation and poesis. A book, Love Is Here To Slay, compiling a number of these works was released at the end of 2020.
As well as the sacredeness in extremes, Eleni's further work in photography seeks of worship to the in everyday as well as revelations in everyday encounters
